Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in analyzing the explainability of AI networks and promoting them from the perspective of biological neuroscience.   Specifically, I am interested in the following areas: 

  • Brain-inspired neural networks: analyzing and designing new neural networks by incorporating findings from biological neuroscience research.
    •   
  • One-class-incremental continual learning of neural networks. 
  • Modular Network design and improvement.
  • Robustness and interpretability of neural networks.
